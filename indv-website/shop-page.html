The shop page will be clean and simple to maneuver through. It will be a basic grid of product pictures with their prices and titles below them. In terms of the CSS, I want there to be an even and decent amount of padding (white space) around the grid of images. This way, the page will look clean and professional. I also want to add a small product description underneath each image, so that the customer can get the general idea of what the product is without having to manuever to a different page. I want the user to be able to shop for a long time and be given as much information up front about each product. There will also be a shopping cart in which they can place items in their cart before proceeding to the purchase and downloading process.
